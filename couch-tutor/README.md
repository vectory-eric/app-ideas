# Couch Tutor

> "Learn English From What You're Already Watching"

Connects to what you're watching on Netflix/YouTube. Pulls the transcript and builds micro-lessons around slang, fast speech, and cultural references from that specific episode.

## Target User
Immigrants who watch English-language TV daily but passively

## iOS Mechanics
- SharePlay integration for learning with friends
- Now Playing integration to detect what's playing
- Dynamic Island showing "phrase from this scene"

## Biggest Risk
Content licensing / IP issues with referencing specific shows. Audio fingerprinting is an App Store gray area. Could work as a YouTube companion (public transcripts).
