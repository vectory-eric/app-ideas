# Couch Tutor

> "Learn English From What You're Already Watching"

## What
Connects to what you're watching on Netflix/YouTube. Pulls transcripts and builds micro-lessons around slang, fast speech, and cultural references from that specific episode.

## Why
Immigrants watch English TV daily but passively. This turns passive consumption into active learning.

## How
- **iOS:** SharePlay, Now Playing integration, Dynamic Island ("phrase from this scene")
- **Content:** Episode transcripts with slang/idiom breakdowns
