# Between the Lines

> "Read What Americans Actually Mean"

A reading companion app. Paste or screenshot any real-world English text — an email from your kid's school, a news article, a work Slack message — and it explains **tone, subtext, formality level, and cultural assumptions**. Not just translation, but comprehension.

## Target User
Immigrant parents, office workers

## Key Insight
Every immigrant screenshots confusing emails and texts friends asking "what does this mean?" This app productizes that existing behavior.

## iOS Mechanics
- Share Sheet extension (share from any app)
- Screenshot OCR via VisionKit
- Action Button shortcut to quick-capture

## Monetization
- Free for 3 analyses/day, $5/month unlimited

## Moat
- Builds over time as it learns your context (workplace, kids' school, city, level)

## Biggest Risk
Overlaps with what ChatGPT does if prompted correctly. Moat must come from UX speed and contextual memory.
