# WorkSpeak

> "English for Your Actual Job"

## What
Vertical-specific English training. Select your industry and get scenarios you'll actually face: calling in sick, asking for time off, understanding safety briefings, writing professional emails.

## Why
Employers in the U.S. spend billions on ESL training. One vertical (restaurant/hospitality) has massive workforce, well-defined vocabulary, and high turnover = constant new users.

## How
- **iOS:** App Clips for employer onboarding (QR in break room, no download), offline mode
- **Content:** Short, audio-first lessons built around rehearsable scripts
- **Distribution:** B2B to restaurant chains — App Clips bypass App Store discovery
