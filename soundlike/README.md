# SoundLike

> "Accent Coaching That Doesn't Judge"

## What
Pronunciation and accent reduction with phoneme-level feedback. Adapts to which English you're targeting (American Midwest, Australian, British RP, etc.)

## Why
Professionals feel self-conscious in meetings due to accent. Feedback focuses on "how to adjust" not "you're wrong."

## How
- **iOS:** CoreML pronunciation model, ARKit front camera for mouth/tongue visualization

## Status
Deprioritized — ELSA Speak has $27M+ funding and this positioning.
