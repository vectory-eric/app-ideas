# MeetingPrep

> "Never Be Lost in a Work Meeting Again"

## What
Paste a meeting agenda or calendar invite. The app pre-teaches likely vocabulary, common phrases for that meeting type, and gives ready-made sentence starters.

## Why
White-collar immigrants lose confidence in meetings due to unfamiliar jargon and fast-paced discussion.

## How
- **iOS:** EventKit calendar integration, 15-min prep push notifications, post-meeting logging via Shortcuts
