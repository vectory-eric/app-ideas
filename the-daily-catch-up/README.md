# The Daily Catch-Up

> "Your Personalized News, Deconstructed"

A daily news briefing that takes 3-5 real headlines from AP/Reuters and presents each article in layers: simplified summary -> full article -> deep dive on hard phrases -> listening version at adjustable speed. News consumption *is* the lesson.

## Target User
Educated immigrants who want to follow current events but find news English overwhelming

## Key Insight
News is something you want to consume anyway, so the motivation problem evaporates. The layered difficulty design respects the user's intelligence in a way Duolingo never does.

## iOS Mechanics
- Lock Screen widgets with "today's phrase from the news"
- Background audio for commute listening
- Siri Shortcuts for "give me today's English lesson"

## Monetization
- Subscription — fresh content daily is a natural fit

## Biggest Risk
Content licensing for real news. Could sidestep with public domain or AP-licensed content. News apps are a retention graveyard.
