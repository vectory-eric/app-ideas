# Overheard

> "Learn From the English Around You"

## What
Tap a widget when you overhear something you didn't understand. Speak or type what you heard. The app identifies the phrase, explains meaning/tone/formality/regional usage, and builds a personal vocab deck from your real life.

## Why
Vocabulary sourced from your actual encounters is more memorable and relevant than textbook lists.

## How
- **iOS:** Widget + Shortcuts ("Hey Siri, I heard something"), on-device whisper-model transcription
