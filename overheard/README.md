# Overheard

> "Learn From the English Around You"

Tap a widget when you overhear something you didn't understand. Speak or type what you *think* you heard. The app identifies the phrase, explains meaning/tone/formality/regional usage, and builds a personal "encountered vocabulary" deck sourced from your real life.

## Target User
Any plateau-stage immigrant

## iOS Mechanics
- Widget + Shortcuts integration ("Hey Siri, I heard something")
- On-device whisper-model transcription

## Biggest Risk
Low build risk (weekend-to-two-week MVP). Retention risk without a strong review loop.
