# Civic English

> "Pass the Test, Understand the System"

## What
Bureaucratic survival English: citizenship tests, parent-teacher conferences, DMV, healthcare forms, tax documents, jury duty. Includes real document templates to practice filling out.

## Why
High-intent users — people will pay if it helps them pass a citizenship interview or navigate government systems.

## How
- **iOS:** VisionKit document scanner for real forms, offline mode for government offices
