# Rewind

> "Replay Your Real Life in English"

Uses iPhone microphone (with consent) to passively capture audio snippets throughout your day, then surfaces moments where you missed something or could've responded better. A "daily debrief" that transcribes, highlights idioms/slang, and lets you practice alternate responses.

## Target User
Immigrant workers in service, healthcare, retail

## iOS Mechanics
- Background audio + on-device Speech Recognition (SpeechFramework)
- Live Activities showing "phrases captured today"

## Biggest Risk
Privacy perception is a minefield. App Store review will scrutinize background recording. Needs tight consent UX and on-device-only processing.

## Status
Deprioritized — too much privacy risk for the value delivered. Months of App Store review battles likely.
