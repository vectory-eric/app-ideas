# Rewind

> "Replay Your Real Life in English"

## What
Passive audio capture throughout your day, then surfaces moments where you missed something or could've responded better. A daily debrief with transcription and practice.

## Why
Real-world immersion is the best teacher — this captures and reviews it.

## How
- **iOS:** Background audio + on-device SpeechFramework, Live Activities
